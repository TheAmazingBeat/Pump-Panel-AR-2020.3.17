Concept

An AR application to provide a new experience in training firefighters to use the Fire Engine's Pump Panel. It is developed in Unity on the Lumin OS (Magic Leap One) platform. The app is divided into two sections: Informational & Simulation.

Informational

The app uses Magic Leap's Image Tracking technology to detect an image of a pump panel and displays a user interface that displays information about each component of the panel.
